About Me

I've undertaken various projects and coursework that have honed my skills in software development, including creating applications with Django, Flask, and Java. I've also ventured into the world of database management, gaining proficiency in SQL.

Outside of academia, I've excelled in various roles, including as a Sales Manager at Virginia ABC, where I demonstrated exceptional leadership and multitasking abilities. I've also completed a web development internship, where I worked on projects supporting corporate strategy and honed my skills in web development, including building a personal portfolio website like this one.

As I continue to tread the path of learning and exploration, I eagerly anticipate the challenges and triumphs that lie ahead. Each experience is shaping a future that amalgamates my diverse interests and ambitions into a meaningful and impactful journey.

Advisor Scheduler - Spring 2023

Project Description: In Spring 2023, I took on the challenge of creating an Advisor Scheduler using the Django Framework. By implementing a user-friendly interface, students can now conveniently schedule appointments through Google login authentication. This system ensures conflict-free scheduling and offers a seamless experience for both advisors and students, enhancing academic support and efficiency.
